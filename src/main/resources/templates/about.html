About GymProgress Tracker

This project is designed to help you track and improve your fitness journey.

  • Track Workouts: Log exercises, sets, reps, weights, and time.
  • Body Measurements: Keep track of body measurements over time.
  • Interactive Charts: View progress with dynamic graphs using Chart.js.
  • Integration: Connect with APIs like Fitbit or Google Fit.
  • Secure: Your data is safe with Spring Security.

Built with Spring Boot, Thymeleaf, and PostgreSQL.
